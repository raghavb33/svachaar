The Pickle Problem

Traditional pickles are beloved, but they're not built for a modern, mobile, health-conscious life. The ₹18,000 Crore market is stuck in the past.

Key Pain Points

  • Breakable & Bulky: Fragile glass jars are heavy, leak-prone, and impossible to travel with.
  • Unhealthy: Most are loaded with palm oil, excess salt, and artificial preservatives like sodium benzoate.
  • No Portion Control: Large 1kg jars mean no convenience for students, hostellers, or single professionals.
  • Inflexible: Seen only as a condiment, not a versatile spread, marinade, or cooking base.

Our Solution: SVACHAAR

  • No Glass, No Leaks: We use lightweight, travel-safe, leak-proof pouches.
  • Clean & Healthy: 100% free of artificial preservatives and palm oil. Small-batch freshness.
  • Protein-Infused: Introducing India's first protein-infused pickles for a healthy, guilt-free indulgence.
  • Multi-Functional: Designed to be a spread, a marinade, or a cooking base.

Freshness-First: Process & Packaging

Our design is not just a look; it's a promise of quality, portability, and sustainability.

Manufacturing & Sourcing

  • Small-Batch Production: We use our own homemade manufacturing unit, making fresh batches to order.
  • Traceable Sourcing: Ingredients are sourced from their native regions (e.g., Mango from UP/Andhra, Gongura from Telangana).
  • No Nasty Stuff: No artificial preservatives, no palm oil, no chemicals. We prioritize local farmers.

Packaging & Sustainability

  • Travel-Safe Pouches: Our core feature. Biodegradable, food-grade laminate pouches with anti-leak seals.
  • No More Glass: Eliminates breakage risk, lowers freight cost, and reduces our carbon footprint.
  • Portion Control: 50g, 250g, and 500g resealable zipper packs to maintain freshness.
