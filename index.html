The Problem We Saw üßê

The Indian pickle market is massive, yet **75% of it is unorganized.** It's fragmented, local, and has failed to innovate for the modern consumer. The market is stuck between two bad options:

  • Bulky Glass Jars: Heavy, inconvenient, breakable, and impossible to travel with. They are designed for a pantry, not a backpack or a suitcase.
  • Health Concerns: Mass-market brands are loaded with excess salt, palm oil, and artificial preservatives (like Sodium Benzoate) to extend shelf life.
  • Inflexibility: Traditional pickle is just a condiment. It's not seen as a versatile ingredient for today's quick-cooking generation.

What We Solve, and How üí°

SVACHAAR was built to solve the core needs of the modern consumer: **Convenience, Health, and Versatility.**

  • How we solve Convenience: We ditched glass. Our lightweight, leak-proof, multi-layer pouches come in **custom sizes**‚Äîfrom 50g "Travel Packs" for students to 5kg "Chef Packs" for HORECA.
  • How we solve Health: We have a "Clean Label" guarantee. **No artificial preservatives. No palm oil.** We use premium healthy oils and even created a "Protein+" line for a healthy, ready-to-eat snack.
  • How we solve Versatility: We "adapted" the pickle. Our "Chef Paste" line turns pickle into a 1-minute marinade, a sandwich spread, or a cooking base.

Our 3 Pillars of Total Convenience

1. Packaging Convenience

Zero Glass, Zero Rigid Plastic. We use premium, lightweight, and leak-proof flexible pouches. They are travel-safe, reduce shipping costs, and are better for the planet. No more breakage, no more leaks.

2. Health Convenience

A "Clean Label" Guarantee. Enjoy authentic flavour with no artificial preservatives and no palm oil. We use premium, health-forward oils (like rice bran or olive oil) and natural preservation methods.

3. Application Convenience

The Pickle, Adapted. We've transformed the pickle from just a side-dish into a versatile cooking ingredient. Our "Pickle Paste" line saves critical prep time for home cooks and professional chefs alike.

Our Go-to-Market Strategy

We are launching as a digital-first, high-margin brand. We will avoid the saturated General Trade (kirana) channel to protect our premium positioning.

Phase 1: Launch (D2C & B2B)

D2C: Launch our e-commerce website, targeting students and health-conscious homes with digital ads.

B2B: Deploy a direct sales team to build a stable, bulk revenue base from HORECA clients using our "Chef-Line".

Phase 2: Scale (Marketplaces)

Online: Expand to key online marketplaces like Amazon, BigBasket, and other specialty food platforms to increase reach.

Corporate: Launch our "Corporate Gifting" vertical by partnering with B2B gifting houses.

Phase 3: Omnichannel (Selective MT)

Retail: After establishing brand equity, we will enter selective Modern Trade (supermarkets) as a premium, differentiated brand in gourmet aisles.
