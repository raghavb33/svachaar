Brand Story

Svachaar began as a single jar on a wooden shelf — a jar that carried summer, memory and the warmth of home. We source regional ingredients and work with small producers to preserve authentic methods while adapting packaging and formats for modern life. Our jars are recyclable glass, recipes are free from preservatives, and every batch is made with care.

Vision

To become India's most loved flavor brand that bridges tradition and contemporary convenience — making regional tastes accessible across kitchens, cafes and fitness plates worldwide.

  • Scale small-batch producers while maintaining quality
  • Introduce Indian flavors to global palates
  • Keep production sustainable and preservative-free
